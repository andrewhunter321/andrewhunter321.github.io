About Me

Hi! I’m Andrew Hunter, a junior software engineer with a love for tech, music, and adventure. Here’s a little more about me.

Where it Began

    Computers and technology have always been a huge interest of mine since a young age. What initially began with gaming, especially Redstone in Minecraft, and even programming my own games on Roblox. It was never anything I thought too deeply into, until I went to Junior High School and attended an afterschool programming club where we got a hold of a BBC MicroBit, which was programmable with Python.

    This built a strong foundation for my love of programming, and I wrote my first game fully in python. Again, another break was taken from programming until I started my foundation degree and had a programming module. In this we used C# with visual studio, and it was my first introduction to user interfaces outside of programming CLIs.

    At this stage, I was never really sure about a career in programming, and kept it fully hobby-based. However, In 2023 I started my bachelors degree in Computing, which offered an apprenticeship route and I secured full-time employment with Trace First - an animal healthcare company.

    Although terrifying at first, I finally got to see how a team of software engineers group together to produce superb software, and was quickly introduced to Ruby on Rails and Javascript with React - and I realised that all the worrys I had before were irrelevant.

    Although I'm at a very early stage of my career, I am so excited to see what the future beholds!
